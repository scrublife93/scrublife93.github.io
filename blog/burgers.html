Our Ranking Methodology

Finding the best burger in Zurich isn't just about hype; it's about the details. We personally visit every spot anonymously, paying full price to ensure an unbiased experience.

Our scoring system breaks down the perfect burger into three pillars:

  • The Bun: Must be toasted, sturdy enough to hold the juices, yet soft enough to bite through without resistance.
  • The Meat: The star of the show. We look for a deep maillard crust (especially on smash burgers), proper seasoning, and high-quality beef blends.
  • Balance: Sauce, cheese, and toppings should complement, not overpower. Ratio is key.
